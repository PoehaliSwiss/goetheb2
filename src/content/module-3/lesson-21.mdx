German academic sentences are like a nut.

* **The Shell:** Adjectives, relative clauses, prepositional phrases.
* **The Nut (Core):** Subject + Conjugated Verb.

**The Algorithm:**

1. **Stop reading linearly.**
2. Find the **Conjugated Verb** (Position 2 or End).
3. Find the **Subject** (Who is doing it?).
4. Everything else is just "decoration" (Time, Place, Manner).
