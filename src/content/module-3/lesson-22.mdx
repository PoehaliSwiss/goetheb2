## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "Hook" System

Imagine the gap is a missing puzzle piece. It has "Hooks" that must fit the surrounding sentences.

**Look for these Hooks:**

1. **Pronouns:** If the sentence after the gap starts with "**Er**...", the missing sentence must mention a **Man** or a **Masculine Noun**.
2. **Demonstratives:** If the sentence after the gap says "**Diese** Ma√ünahme...", the missing sentence must describe a **measure** (Ma√ünahme).
3. **Connectors:**
* *Dennoch/Trotzdem:* The missing sentence must express a **Contrast** to the previous one.
* *Deshalb/Folglich:* The missing sentence must be the **Cause** of the next one.
