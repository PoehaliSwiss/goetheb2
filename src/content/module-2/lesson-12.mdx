## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The Two-Part Bridges

Double Connectors connect two main clauses (or parts of clauses). They create a "Bracket of Logic".

| Connector Pair | Meaning | Logic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Zwar ..., aber ...** | True/Admittedly ..., but ... | Concession (Yes, X is true, but Y is more important). |
| **Nicht nur ..., sondern auch ...** | Not only ..., but also ... | Addition (X is good, AND Y is good). |
| **Sowohl ... als auch ...** | Both ... and ... | Addition (Just like 'und', but stronger). |
| **Entweder ... oder ...** | Either ... or ... | Alternative (Pick one). |
| **Weder ... noch ...** | Neither ... nor ... | Negative Addition (No X and No Y). |
| **Einerseits ..., andererseits ...** | On the one hand ..., on the other ... | Comparison/Contrast. |

**Word Order Alert:**

* *Zwar* usually stands at **Position 1** or **Position 3** (after verb).
* *Aber/Sondern/Oder* usually stand at **Position 0** (connecting the clauses).
* *Einerseits/Andererseits* usually occupy **Position 1**, forcing the Verb to **Position 2**.
