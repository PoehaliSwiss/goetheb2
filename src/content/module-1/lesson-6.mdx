## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "Functional Verb" Mechanism

In "Normal German" (B1), the **Verb** carries the meaning (*entscheiden, kritisieren, helfen*).
In "Official German" (B2+), we often split the meaning into a **Noun** + a **Functional Verb**.

* **Noun:** Carries the *meaning*.
* **Verb:** Carries the *grammar* (Person, Tense), but loses its original meaning.

**Example:**

* *Simple:* Wir **entscheiden** uns. (Verb carries meaning).
* *NVV:* Wir **treffen** eine **Entscheidung**.
* *Treffen* normally means "to meet/hit". Here, it just means "to do/make".
* *Entscheidung* holds the content.



**Why use this?**

1. **Precision:** You can add adjectives to the noun (*eine **schwere** Entscheidung treffen*).
2. **Passive:** Easier to use in passive constructions (*Eine Entscheidung wurde getroffen*).
3. **Register:** It sounds more objective and academic.
