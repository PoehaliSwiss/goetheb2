## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "TeKaMoLo" Law

In B1, you learned "Verb at Position 2".
In B2, you must master the **Mittelfeld** (the middle field between the verbs).
German arranges information by **Importance** and **Type**, not randomly.

The Golden Rule for the Mittelfeld: **Te ‚Äì Ka ‚Äì Mo ‚Äì Lo**

1. **Te**mporal (Wann? / Time)
2. **Ka**usal (Warum? / Reason)
3. **Mo**dal (Wie? / Manner)
4. **Lo**kal (Wo? / Place)

**Example:**

* *Basic:* Ich fahre morgen wegen der Arbeit schnell nach Berlin.
* **Te:** morgen (Time)
* **Ka:** wegen der Arbeit (Reason)
* **Mo:** schnell (Manner)
* **Lo:** nach Berlin (Place)

**Position 1 (The Spotlight):**
You can move **one** element to Position 1 to emphasize it. This pushes the Subject to Position 3.

* *Neutral:* Ich fahre **morgen** nach Berlin.
* *Emphasis on Time:* **Morgen** fahre ich nach Berlin.
* *Emphasis on Reason:* **Wegen der Arbeit** fahre ich morgen nach Berlin.

---

## 2. üìò Deep Theory: The "Dativ vor Akkusativ" Rule

If you have two objects in the Mittelfeld:

1. **Noun + Noun:** Dative comes **before** Akkusativ.
* *Ich gebe **dem Mann** (Dat) **das Buch** (Akk).*


2. **Pronoun + Noun:** Pronoun comes **before** Noun.
* *Ich gebe **es** (Pronoun) **dem Mann** (Noun).*


3. **Pronoun + Pronoun:** Akkusativ comes **before** Dative.
* *Ich gebe **es** (Akk) **ihm** (Dat).*

## 4. ‚úçÔ∏è Professional Grammar: Nominalization (Intro)

Goethe B2 loves **Nominalization**. This means turning a whole sentence (Verbal Style) into a Noun Phrase (Nominal Style). This is essential for the "Forum Post" introduction.

* **Verbal (B1):** *Weil es viel regnet, bleiben wir zu Hause.*
* **Nominal (B2):** *Wegen des vielen Regens bleiben wir zu Hause.*

**Transformation Table:**

* *weil* (Connector) -> *wegen* + Genitive (Preposition)
* *obwohl* (Connector) -> *trotz* + Genitive (Preposition)
* *damit* (Connector) -> *zu* + Dative (Preposition)
